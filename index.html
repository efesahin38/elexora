Frequently Asked Questions

Find the answers to the most common questions here.

How do we get started?

First, we listen to you and conduct a free analysis meeting (online or face-to-face). After understanding your challenges, we present a roadmap with the most suitable solutions and budget. Once approved, we get to work immediately.

How long does custom software development take?

While it varies depending on the scope, simple integrations take 1-2 weeks, and more comprehensive automation or CRM/TAMS panels are completed in 4-8 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during the planning phase.

Can features be added to the software later?

Absolutely. All systems we develop have a modular and scalable architecture. As your business grows or new needs arise, we can quickly integrate new features into your software.

Do you provide warranty or technical support?

All of our coded software products are covered under a 1-year warranty against any bugs or system errors.
